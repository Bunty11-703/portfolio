Who I am

About me

I'm a fresh Applied AI and Machine Learning engineer from Bangalore who believes that a model is only as good as the experience it creates for the person using it. I don't just train models — I build complete systems: APIs, dashboards, user flows, and explainability layers.

My work spans NLP-based information retrieval (resume analysis, semantic matching), supervised ML for prediction (disease classification, credit risk), and multimodal AI (deepfake detection). Every project I build is deployed and publicly accessible.

I'm drawn to AI problems where the stakes are real — healthcare, information verification, hiring — because those are the domains where explainability and trust matter most. I want to build AI that users can actually rely on, not just benchmark on paper.
